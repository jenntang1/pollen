ABOUT:

This idea was made in partnership between Jennifer Tang and Flavio Espinoza. It came to us during a time of collapse, and we wanted to provide people with digital tools to engage with the world around them. It's a conundrum, but the hope for this application is to build interest on niche topics and knowledge-share with the world. Knowledge, like pollen, spreads in the most mysterious of ways, without any real ways to see it but plenty of ways to track it. See some of our archived stories here:
